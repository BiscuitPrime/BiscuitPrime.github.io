About Me

Hello, my name is Henri NOMICO, I am a french 3rd-year engineering student at Télécom Sudparis, within the Video Game Development Major 💻
I am currently learning many programming languages such as C#, C++, C, Java, Python, Bash, PHP, Javascript,HTML, as well as maths, telecomunication, physics, sustainable development and management.
While I always enjoyed creating universes and imaginary stories, I became enthralled by video-game making as I learned to code.
As such, I continued to learn by participating in GameJams and turning my School projects into games.
While also developing on my free time, I also began to learn 3D modelling, using my models in my own games, animated shorts or for art projects.
I yearn to be a Game Developer in the future !

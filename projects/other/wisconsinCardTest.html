Wisconsin Sorting Card Test

The Wisconsin Sorting Card Test is a neuropsychological test of set-shifting, which is the capability to show flexibility when exposed to changes in reinforcement.

Wisconsin Sorting Card Test

The Wisconsin Sorting Card Test is a task I was assigned to do during a technical test. Afterwards, I decided to pimp it up and release it.
Made under 3 hours in Unity, the test can be played on your browser. While not the most engaging program I ever created, I still enjoyed the process.

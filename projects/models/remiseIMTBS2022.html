Graduation Ceremony IMTBS 2022

This animation was made as a prestation for the IMTBSschool, for their 2022 graduation ceremony. While not a personal project, the animation was made under the 3D modelling club of TSP, of which I was the President during the time, "Le RIG". The ceremony's theme was : "Around the world".
During this project, I was the Project Manager and 3D Designer/Animator.

Fly Me to The Moon

In the year 2043, Earth is under siege from an army of aliens. You are the captain of the Blue Phoenix, a ship designed to ferry survivor to the Moon's holdout, last remaining bastion of Human resistance. Alas, your ship has been targeted and hordes of dealy aliens are swarming towards your vessel. Before your departure, Professor Hoffren has told you about a mysterious new technology : the Yeetor, a device capable of turning the sacrifice of one of your crewmates into powerful boosts. Can you make it Moon-side ?

Fly Me to The Moon

A point-and-click game made in Unity as patrt of the 2020's UNIJAM, a jam organized by the ENSIIE and Télécom SudParis schools. During the project, I was the Sound Designer and one of the graphists.
My first ever gamejam, it allowed me to discover the inner workings of game-making in a controlled environment, something that would build the basis for my next gamejams. It also allowed me to touch a small bit at the often forgotten aspect of Game Design : Sound Design.

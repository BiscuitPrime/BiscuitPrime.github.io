Steppe by Step

A team of hunter-gatherers of the Magdalenian period are on the hunt ! A herd of reindeers is known to pass nearby, a valuable target ! Help them find the pack and feed their group ! Game made as part of the Etiolles 2022 Archeaological GameJam

Steppe by Step

Steppe by Step is an RTS-style game where you command a pack of Magdalenian-era hunter-gatherers. Your goal is to hunt a reindeer herd. This game was made as part of the 2022 Etiolles Game Jam that took place in Evry, France, from the October 8 to the October 10. This Game Jam was organised to celebrate the 50th anniversary of the archeological excavation site of Etiolles, Essone. During the project, I was a Developer and 3D Artist.
This project was very interesting, as it required us to create a game that was accurate to the Magdalenian era, an era completely unbeknownst to me before. As such, we worked in tandem with professional archeologists. Furthermore, unlike my previous gamejams, we had dedicated professional sound designers during the project, which allowed us to have much more complex sounds and adaptative musics.

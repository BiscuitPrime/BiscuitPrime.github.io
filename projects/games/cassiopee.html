Le Dernier Recours

On the 20th april 2052 an unknown ship suddenly appears in Earth's orbit. The Global Space Recon Agency (GSRA) sends one of its best element to investigate : you. The derelict vessel seems abandonned, but all accesses hatches have been unlocked, leaving you free to enter the ship... The only thing you find when entering the abandonned craft is a picture of Earth, devastated and burning. What is the meaning of this picture ? Why is the ship abandonned, left adrift in the coldness of space ? You will need to use every bit of your orientation skills and ingenuity to uncover the truth lying deep inside this dead ship.
Will you manage to lift the veil on all the mysteries hiding in the bowels of this vessel ?

Le Dernier Recours

Le Dernier Recours is a VR project done in Unity using the TraVRsal technology, that allows the player to freely move in 3D space. It was a school project, as part of the Cassiopee projects of our school, Télécom Sudparis. Our project ranked 1st in the Cassiopee competition. During this project, I was a developer as well as lead 3D artist.
This project allowed me to discover not only Virtual Reality development, but also the TraVRsal technology, and the various issues that can arise from using a new and innovative technology. It also allowed me to further develop team skills as well as customer relationship management, as it is one of the first projects were I had a clear, distinct, client that we interacted with.
